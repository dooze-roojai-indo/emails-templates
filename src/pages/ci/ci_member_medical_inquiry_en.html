  2. If pre-existing conditions (as defined in Additional Clause of policy definition of Pre-Existing Condition) are found to have existed before the policy was active, the policy will be canceled and there will be no premium refund.
  3. If pre-existing conditions (as defined in Additional Clause of policy definition of Pre-Existing Condition) are found, but deemed not directly related to the chosen coverage, the Underwriting team will offer some adjustments of the risk assessments, which may impact scope of coverage and premium. Customers can then make a decision whether to proceed or cancel the policy.
